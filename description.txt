# Blocky Outline

**Blocky Outline** is a highly polished, premium client-side Fabric mod for Minecraft that completely transforms how you interact with the world by adding highly customizable block-targeting outlines and solid face fills. Say goodbye to the boring, static default outline—with Blocky Outline, you can fully personalize your targeting visual style to match your aesthetic!

---

## ✨ Features

- **Custom Block Outlines & Face Fills:** Choose between rendering a beautiful custom outline around targeted blocks, a sleek translucent solid face fill, or both at the same time.
- **Dynamic HSL Rainbow Transitions:** Elevate your game with gorgeous, smooth color transitions. Configure a dynamic rainbow effect that shifts through the HSL spectrum with adjustable speeds.
- **Vibrant & Precise Color Customization:** Tweak red, green, blue, and alpha/opacity channels individually to create perfect custom colors for both the outline and the fill.
- **Sodium, Iris & Canvas Compatibility:** Built with advanced modern rendering methods. The mod utilizes a double-faced vertex consumer structure and a micro-inflation factor (`0.005D`) to completely eliminate z-fighting, backface culling, or clipping issues commonly found when using modern performance shaders like Iris and Sodium.
- **Native Keybinding Integration:** Seamlessly integrated into Minecraft’s native control settings. Simply press the **M** key (fully rebindable in the standard Controls options) to open the interactive customization menu screen in-game.
- **Frame-Synchronized Caching:** Implements intelligent rendering pipeline optimization to prevent rendering bugs (such as outline elements getting stuck to the ground), ensuring buttery-smooth performance.
- **Client-Side Only:** Purely client-side! You can join any multiplayer server, vanilla or modded, and enjoy your stunning custom block highlights.

---

## 🛠️ Configuration & Customization

Once you are in-game, simply press **M** to open the customizer dashboard. From there, you can toggle:
1. **Outline Mode:** Adjust the outline width, transparency (alpha), and exact color.
2. **Fill Mode:** Enable or disable targeted block face highlighting, customize its transparency so it doesn't block your view, and configure independent custom colors or HSL rainbow transitions.

---

## 🚀 Installation & Versions

This mod is optimized for **Minecraft 1.21+** (fully verified on **1.21.11**) and requires:
- **Fabric Loader** (>=0.19.2)
- **Fabric API**

Simply drag the `.jar` file into your `mods` folder and you are ready to play!
